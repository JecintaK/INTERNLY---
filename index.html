Internly: Connecting
students to internships

Many students struggle to find internships where they can be able to showcase their skills in
real world projects. Companies struggle to get fresh creative talent that will be a great addition
to their company to enable them to achieve their objectives. Internly aims to connect students
to internships perfect for their skills. Where they get to work on real world projects.
Companies get to select the best- fit interns for their company to ensure they achieve their
objectives.
